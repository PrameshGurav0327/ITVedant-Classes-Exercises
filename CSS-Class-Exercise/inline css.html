MARVEL

Marvel and the MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe) are related but different concepts:

Marvel Comics: Marvel, originally known as Timely Publications and later as Atlas Comics, is a comic book publisher founded in 1939. It created a vast universe of characters and stories, including iconic superheroes like Spider-Man, Iron Man, the X-Men, and the Avengers.

Marvel Entertainment: This is the overarching company that manages the Marvel brand, including Marvel Comics, Marvel Television, Marvel Animation, and other media ventures.
